What is Upkit?

Upkit is a company that provides boxed experiences.

These would be in the form of all-in-one kits such as:

Baking

A kit including: Standup Mixer, Bowls, Spoons, Molds, Tins, etc. 

Cleaning

A kit including: Hoover, Steam Cleaner, Wet/Dry Vac, etc 

Kids

A kit including: Footballs, Backgarden tennis, Painting kits, etc 

All in one kits

All you could need

How will it work?

Sign Up

Sign up for a yearly membership to gain access to the kits. This covers the insurance on all the products used. 

Select Kit

Browse and select a kit from the website.

Weekend delivery

Enjoy your kit for the weekend as it's delivered on Friday/Saturday.

Return your kit

Pickup is arranged for after the weekend.
